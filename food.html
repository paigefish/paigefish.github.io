Beechers

I have grown up in a family with the motto family is food and food is family. Whenever, we get together, there is always food involved. Especially, I love when I come home to my mom's cooking. Whether its just family dinner with the four of us or a big family gathering, she always makes food that tastes amazing. I love when she makes her small homemade chicken pot pies or her sticky chicken wings.

Addintionally, I huge tradition that is being past down is baking. We have some small pastries that we make that take a long time and we typically only make once a year. I was lucky enough to get to know my great grandmother who lived till she was 97 and didn't pass away until I was around 17. I learned my baking skills from her and to this day, I still bake with my mom and grandma when I can at home.
