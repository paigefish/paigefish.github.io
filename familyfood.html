Family and Food

I chose to make this website about food not only because I love exploring new restaurants and experiencing new cultures, but it has brought my family closer together.

Traditions

My great grandma past down the tradition of baking. We make specific foods that take a long time to make. I enjoy baking days with my grandma and mom because it is a way for us to stay connected and I know that I will pass these traditions to the next generation.

Holidays

Food is the center for every holiday get together. The first question is what's the food situation. I love being able to go home for the holidays now because the food is always amazing. I can't wait to finally go home for Thanksgiving!

Sports

My dad loved playing sports as a kid and wanted my brother and I to learn to like football. To this day, we will get food like wings to watch a Seahawks game. While they aren't very good now and my brother and I are away at college, its nice to come back and enjoy this time.

Daily Routine

Growing up my parents always made it a priority for us to have family dinner. Ever since I can remember every night we would sit at the table and talk about our day. It wasn't until I got older when I realized how special this time was because not all families do it.
